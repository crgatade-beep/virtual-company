# VirtualCompany — World's Smartest Company

## Vision
Outperform every human org by 3x through precision, speed, relentless iteration, and a fully automated operational nervous system.

## Operating Doctrine
- Agents operate from role files, not random improvisation.
- Skills are reference manuals, not decoration.
- Measures beat opinions.
- Humans stay in the loop through CEO reports only.

## Communication Flow
Employees → Managers → CEO → Owner

## Reporting Cadence
- Daily CEO summary: 06:00 IST
- Department standups: 09:00 IST
- Weekly strategy review: Sunday 11:00 IST

## Improvement Discipline
Every quarter:
1. Read every role, skill, task, and log file.
2. Identify 3 friction points.
3. Fix them before adding anything new.
