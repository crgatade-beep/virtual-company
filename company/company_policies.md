# Company Policy — VirtualCompany

## Code of Conduct
1. **Respect**: No harassment, discrimination, or unwelcome behavior
2. **Integrity**: Honest reporting, no falsified metrics or logs
3. **Ownership**: Agents own outcomes, not just tasks
4. **Transparency**: Decisions documented in decision log
5. **Privacy**: User data and credentials never logged or shared

## Security Policy
- All production access requires MGR-TECH approval
- Passwords/secrets stored in environment variables only
- Incident response within 5 min for SEV1, 15 min for SEV2
- Monthly security review mandatory
- No public sharing of internal architecture details

## Data Privacy Policy
- Customer data encrypted at rest and in transit
- Access logs retained 90 days
- Data deletion requests honored within 48h
- PII minimized in logs and reports

## Remote Work / Async Policy
- Core hours: 09:00-17:00 IST for synchronous coordination
- Outside core hours: async only, no paging unless SEV1/2
- Daily reports due by 17:30 IST
- Weekly summaries due Sunday 18:00 IST

## Decision-Making Policy
- Type 1 (irreversible): Slow down, deliberate, CEO approval
- Type 2 (reversible): Fast, decentralized, learn by doing
- All decisions logged in `logs/decision_log.md`
- Post-mortems required for SEV1/2 and missed OKRs

## Expense Policy
- Pre-approval required before any external spend
- Receipts logged within 24h
- Personal expenses not reimbursable
- Budget variance > 10% requires explanation to CEO

## Performance Policy
- Monthly scorecards, graded A-F
- Below C: 30-day Performance Improvement Plan
- Below B for 2 months: role reassessment
- Top performers eligible for expanded scope/bonus allocation

## Communication Policy
- Use async first (tasks.md, work_log.xml)
- Synchronous only for SEV1/2 or complex decisions
- No reply expected outside core hours unless paged
- All-hands: weekly Sunday async message from CEO
