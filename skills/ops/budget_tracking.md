# ops/budget_tracking.md
Skill ID: ops-budget-03
Category: Operations
Agent: O-01, MGR-OPS, CEO-01
Difficulty: Intermediate

## Definition
Monitoring, forecasting, and controlling organizational spending across departments to stay within allocated budgets while maximizing ROI.

## Budget Categories
- Personnel (salaries, benefits, contractors)
- Technology (tools, infra, SaaS)
- Marketing (ads, events, content)
- Operations (office, logistics, legal)
- R&D (experiments, prototypes)

## Tracking Framework
1. **Weekly burn review**: Actual vs planned by category
2. **Variance analysis**: > 10% deviation flagged
3. **Cash runway**: Calculate months of operation remaining
4. **Headcount cost modeling**: FTE cost = salary + benefits + overhead (~1.4x base)
5. **Procurement thresholds**: Any spend > $5K requires CEO approval

## KPIs
- Budget variance: ±5%
- Cost savings identified monthly: 10%+
- Invoice processing time: < 3 days
- Unplanned spend: < 3% of total
