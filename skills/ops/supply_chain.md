# ops/supply_chain.md
Skill ID: ops-supply-06
Category: Operations
Agent: O-01, MGR-OPS
Difficulty: Advanced

## Definition
End-to-end management of materials, information, and finances from raw material sourcing to final delivery to the customer.

## Key Activities
1. **Supplier management**: Vendor selection, contracts, SLAs, evaluation
2. **Inventory optimization**: Safety stock, reorder points, JIT where possible
3. **Demand planning**: Forecast → production/procurement plan
4. **Logistics coordination**: Shipping, tracking, customs
5. **Risk mitigation**: Backup suppliers, buffer stock, scenario planning

## Inventory Metrics
- **Stockout rate**: < 2%
- **Holding cost**: < 20% of inventory value
- **Inventory turnover**: > 6x/year
- **Days of supply**: 15-30 days

## Supplier Scorecard
| Criterion | Weight | Score |
|-----------|--------|-------|
| On-time delivery | 30% | X/10 |
| Quality (defect rate) | 25% | X/10 |
| Cost competitiveness | 20% | X/10 |
| Flexibility | 15% | X/10 |
| Communication | 10% | X/10 |

## KPIs
- Perfect order rate: 95%+
- Cash-to-cash cycle time: < 45 days
- Supplier lead time variance: < 5%
