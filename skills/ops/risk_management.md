# ops/risk_management.md
Skill ID: ops-risk-04
Category: Operations
Agent: MGR-OPS, CEO-01
Difficulty: Advanced

## Definition
Proactive identification, assessment, and mitigation of threats that could derail operations, damage reputation, or cause financial loss.

## Risk Categories
1. **Operational**: Process failures, system outages, supply chain breaks
2. **Financial**: Cash flow, fraud, market crashes
3. **Reputational**: PR crises, brand damage, social media backlash
4. **Compliance**: Regulatory violations, legal exposure, data breaches
5. **Strategic**: Wrong bets, missed trends, competitive displacement

## Risk Assessment Matrix
```
Impact:   Low    Medium    High
Prob  Low        Low       Medium
       Medium    Medium    High
       High      High      CRITICAL
```

## Response Protocol
- **CRITICAL**: Escalate CEO immediately, daily standups until resolved
- **HIGH**: Manager owns, daily progress reports to CEO
- **MEDIUM**: Team lead owns, weekly updates
- **LOW**: Monitor log, no active mitigation needed

## KPIs
- Risks identified before incident: 80%+
- Mean time to detect (MTTD): < 1 hour
- Mean time to resolve (MTTR): < 4 hours
- Critical incidents per quarter: 0
