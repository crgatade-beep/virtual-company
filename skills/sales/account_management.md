# sales/account_management.md
Skill ID: sales-acct-mgmt-06
Category: Sales
Agent: S-01, S-02
Difficulty: Intermediate

## Definition
Ongoing relationship management with existing customers to maximize retention, upsell, cross-sell, and lifetime value.

## Key Activities
1. **QBRs (Quarterly Business Reviews)**: Scheduled strategic check-ins
2. **Health scoring**: Green/Yellow/Red based on usage, NPS, support tickets
3. **Expansion signals**: Track feature adoption, seat expansion, new use cases
4. **Churn prevention**: Early warning system at 30/60/90 day intervals
5. **Executive mapping**: Know all stakeholders, their goals, their politics

## Health Score Framework
- Green: Active usage, growing spend, promoter NPS
- Yellow: Usage stable, neutral NPS, no expansion
- Red: Usage dropping, detractor NPS, active competitor evaluation

## KPIs
- Retention rate: 90%+
- NPS: 50+
- Expansion revenue: 25% of total
- Average contract value growth: 15% YoY
