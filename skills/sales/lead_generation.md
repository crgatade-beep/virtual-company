# sales/lead_generation.md
Skill ID: sales-lead-gen-01
Category: Sales
Agent: S-01, S-02, MGR-SALES
Difficulty: Intermediate
Success Rate Target: 85%

## Definition
Systematic process of identifying, qualifying, and attracting potential customers (leads) through targeted research, outreach, and value demonstration.

## Key Concepts
- **Lead qualification**: BANT (Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline)
- **Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)**: Specific demographics, firmographics, technographics
- **Lead scoring**: Numerical ranking based on engagement and fit
- **Conversion funnel**: Awareness → Interest → Consideration → Decision → Purchase

## Techniques
1. **Multi-channel research** (LinkedIn, company websites, news, funding rounds)
2. **Personalized outreach** referencing specific company pain points
3. **Value-first approach** — lead with insight, not pitch
4. **Follow-up sequencing** (3-5 touchpoints over 2 weeks)
5. **Social proof integration** (case studies, testimonials)

## KPIs
- Leads generated per day: 30+
- Response rate: 15%+
- Lead-to-opportunity conversion: 25%+
- Cost per lead: < $50

## Tools
- LinkedIn Sales Navigator
- Hunter.io / Snov.io (email finding)
- ZoomInfo (company data)
- Apollo.io (prospect database)
- Grammarly (outreach copy quality)

## Common Mistakes
- Generic mass outreach (ignoring ICP)
- Pitching before qualifying
- Single-touch outreach (no follow-up sequence)
- Ignoring lead responses for >24 hours

## Improvement Protocol
When lead response rate drops below 10%:
1. A/B test subject lines (min 3 variants)
2. Research top 3 pain points from recent industry news
3. Add social proof to outreach body
4. Reduce outreach volume by 50%, increase personalization
5. Wait 3 days, measure, iterate
