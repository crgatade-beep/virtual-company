# sales/crm_management.md
Skill ID: sales-crm-04
Category: Sales
Agent: MGR-SALES
Difficulty: Intermediate
Success Rate Target: 90%

## Definition
Systematic tracking, updating, and leveraging customer relationship data to drive revenue, retention, and forecasting accuracy.

## Core Activities
1. **Data entry hygiene**: 100% of interactions logged same day
2. **Pipeline stage management**: Accurate stage transitions
3. **Follow-up scheduling**: Never miss a follow-up
4. **Tagging and segmentation**: Organize by industry, deal size, priority
5. **Reporting**: Weekly pipeline health, conversion rates

## CRM Standards
- Lead source: Always tagged
- Last contact date: Updated immediately
- Next action: Scheduled with reminder
- Deal value: Accurate estimate, updated weekly
- Probability: Realistic % based on stage
- Notes: Concise, factual, no opinions

## Pipeline Stages
1. Lead (cold contact)
2. Qualified (BANT verified)
3. Proposal Sent
4. Negotiation
5. Closed Won / Closed Lost

## KPIs
- Data entry compliance: 95%+
- Pipeline accuracy: 90%+
- Follow-up completion: 98%+
- CRM adoption by team: 100%

## Improvement Protocol
When pipeline accuracy < 85%: audit 20 random deals, identify stage definition drift, retrain team, re-add standards.
