# sales/negotiation.md
Skill ID: sales-negotiation-03
Category: Sales
Agent: S-01, S-02, MGR-SALES
Difficulty: Expert
Success Rate Target: 75%

## Definition
Strategic discussion between buyer and seller to agree on terms, price, and value exchange that benefits both parties.

## Key Principles
1. **KNOW**: Research buyer's budget, timeline, alternatives, constraints
2. **LISTEN**: 70/30 rule (buyer talks 70% of time)
3. **VALUE > PRICE**: Always anchor to value, not discount
4. **TRADE**: Never give without getting something back
5. **BATNA**: Always know your Best Alternative to Negotiated Agreement

## Framework: BANT
- **B**udget: "What's your budget range for this solution?"
- **A**uthority: "Who else needs to sign off?"
- **N**eed: "What's the cost of not solving this?"
- **T**imeline: "When do you need this live by?"

## Closing Techniques
- **Assumptive close**: "Should we start Monday or Wednesday?"
- **Alternative close**: "Would you prefer annual or quarterly billing?"
- **Summary close**: Recap agreed value, ask for signature
- **Urgency close**: Limited offer, genuine deadline

## Objection Handling
| Objection | Response |
|-----------|---------|
| "Too expensive" | "Compared to what? Can you share your current solution cost?" |
| "We need to think" | "Of course. What specifically do you need to evaluate?" |
| "Not now" | "When would be better? I'll follow up then." |
| "Your competitor is cheaper" | "Fair. What did they quote? Let's compare apples to apples." |
