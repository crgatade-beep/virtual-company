# sales/cold_outreach.md
Skill ID: sales-cold-outreach-02
Category: Sales
Agent: S-01, S-02
Difficulty: Advanced
Success Rate Target: 12%

## Definition
The practice of contacting potential customers who have not previously interacted with your brand, using email, LinkedIn, phone, or social DMs.

## Key Concepts
- **Cold open**: First message in a sequence
- **Breakup email**: Final polite nudge in sequence
- **Pattern interrupt**: Unexpected subject line/hook
- **Social selling**: Using social proof to build trust before pitching

## Opening Lines (Proven)
1. "Quick question about [specific company initiative]..."
2. "Noticed [company] just [recent event] — congrats"
3. "Idea for [department] at [company]: [one-line value]"
4. "[Mutual connection] suggested I reach out"

## Structure
```
1. Subject: 5 words or less
2. Line 1: Personalization hook (1 sentence)
3. Line 2: Problem you solve (1 sentence)
4. Line 3: Social proof (1 sentence)
5. Line 4: Call to action — low friction (1 sentence)
6. Signature: Name, title, company, LinkedIn
```

## Rules
- NEVER use: "just checking in", "circle back", "touch base"
- ALWAYS: Reference a specific recent company event
- ALWAYS: Keep body under 100 words
- ALWAYS: End with a question or CTA
- NEVER: Attach files in first email

## KPIs
- Open rate: 35%+
- Reply rate: 8-15%
- Positive reply rate: 3-5%
- Meetings booked: 2-5%

## Improvement Protocol
When open rate < 30%: test 3 new subject line patterns, 50 emails each, pick winner.
