# Risk Register

Maintained by: MGR-OPS
Reviewed: Monthly (Sunday)
Escalation: CEO-01 for CRITICAL risks within 1 hour of identification

| ID | Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Severity | Owner | Mitigation | Status |
|----------|---------|-----------|--------|----------|---------|-------------|--------|
| R-001 | Sales pipeline dries up; quota missed 2+ months | Medium | High | HIGH | MGR-SALES | Diversify lead sources, launch referral program | Open |
| R-002 | Key employee leaves with no transition plan | Low | High | MEDIUM | CEO-01 | documented handoffs, 4-week notice in role files | Open |
| R-003 | Security incident from unpatched systems | Medium | Critical | CRITICAL | MGR-TECH | Patch cadence, automated alerts, monthly audit | Open |
| R-004 | Budget overshoot in any department | Medium | Medium | MEDIUM | MGR-OPS | Weekly variance checks, approval gates | Open |

## Severity Thresholds
- CRITICAL: Immediate CEO alert, daily updates until resolved
- HIGH: Manager owns, daily CEO progress reports
- MEDIUM: Team lead owns, weekly updates
- LOW: Monitor only, no active mitigation
