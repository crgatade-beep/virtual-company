# Budget Policy — VirtualCompany

## Scope
Covers all spending across Sales, Operations, and Technology.

## Budget Structure
- **Sales monthly budget**: Manager approves up to $10K/month; CEO approval for anything above that.
- **Ops monthly budget**: Up to $25K/month; CEO approval above that.
- **Tech monthly budget**: Up to $30K/month; CEO approval above that.

## Rules
1. All external spend must be pre-approved by the department manager.
2. Any spend over $5K needs a second sign-off from CEO.
3. Recurring subscriptions require documented cancellation notice within 30 days.
4. Every transaction is logged in logs/

## Variance Alerts
- Weekly variance review on Monday.
- Department exceeds 80% of monthly budget: alert CEO immediately.
- Unplanned spend > $3K requires Owner approval.

## Procurement Thresholds
- $500–$1K: Manager approval + Finance review.
- $1K–$5K: Manager + CEO approval.
- $5K+: Board/Owner approval required.

## Currency
All targets and reports are in USD unless explicitly stated otherwise.
